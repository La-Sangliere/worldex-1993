ECONOMY
Overview: Oil is the backbone of the economy and accounts for more than
85% of export earnings and roughly 75% of government revenues. Proved oil
reserves of 3.3 billion barrels should ensure continued output at current
levels for about 25 years. Oil has given Qatar a per capita GDP of about
$17,000, comparable to the leading industrial countries. Production and export
of natural gas is becoming increasingly important.
