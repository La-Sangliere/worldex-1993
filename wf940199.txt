ECONOMY 
Overview: The tourist industry contributes over 50% of GDP. In 1991 over 
3.1 million tourists visited San Marino, 2.7 million of whom were Italians. 
The key industries are wearing apparel, electronics, and ceramics. Main 
agricultural products are wine and cheeses. The per capita level of output and 
standard of living are comparable to northern Italy.
