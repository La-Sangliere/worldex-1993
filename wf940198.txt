ECONOMY
Overview: Agriculture, dominated by banana production, is the most
important sector of the economy. The services sector, based mostly on a
growing tourist industry, is also important. The economy continues to have a
high unemployment rate of 35%-40% because of an overdependence on the
weather-plagued banana crop as a major export earner. Government progress
toward diversifying into new industries has been relatively unsuccessful.
