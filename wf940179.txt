ECONOMY 
Overview: Economic performance is closely tied to the fortunes of the oil 
industry.  Petroleum accounts for more than 85% of export earnings, about 80% 
of government revenues, and roughly 40% of GDP. Oman has proved oil reserves 
of 4 billion barrels, equivalent to about 20 years' supply at the current rate 
of extraction. Agriculture is carried on at a subsistence level and the 
general population depends on imported food.
