ECONOMY 
Overview: The mainstay of Andorra's economy is tourism. An estimated 13 
million tourists visit annually, attracted by Andorra's duty-free status and 
by its summer and winter resorts. The banking sector, with its "tax haven" 
status, also contributes significantly to the economy. Agricultural production 
of cigarettes, cigars, and furniture. Although it is a member of the EC 
customs union, it is unclear what effect the European Single Market will have 
on the advantages Andorra obtains from its duty-free status.
