ECONOMY 
Overview: Tourism, petroleum refining, and offshore finance are the 
mainstays of the economy. The islands enjoy a high per capita income and a 
well-developed infrastructure as compared with other countries in the region. 
Unlike many Latin American countries, the Netherlands Antilles has avoided 
large international debt. Almost all consumer and capital goods are imported, 
with the US being the major supplier.
