ECONOMY 
Overview: The economy is based primarily on agriculture, agro-based 
industry, and merchandising, with tourism and construction assuming increasing 
importance. Agriculture accounts for about 30% of GDP and provides 75% of 
export earnings, while sugar, the chief crop, accounts for almost 40% of hard 
currency earnings. The US, Belize's main trading partner, is assisting in 
efforts to reduce dependency on sugar with an agricultural diversification 
program.
