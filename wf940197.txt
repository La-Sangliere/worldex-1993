ECONOMY 
Overview: Since 1983 the economy has shown an impressive average annual 
growth rate of almost 5% because of strong agricultural and tourist sectors. 
Saint Lucia also possesses an expanding industrial base supported by foreign 
investment in manufacturing and other activities, such as in data processing. 
The economy, however, remains vulnerable because the important agricultural 
sector is dominated by banana production, which is subject to periodic 
droughts and/or tropical storms.
