ECONOMY 
Overview: Tourism is the mainstay of the economy, although offshore 
banking and oil refining and storage are also important. Hotel capacity 
expanded rapidly between 1985 and 1989 and nearly doubled in 1990 alone. 
Unemployment has steadily declined from about 20% in 1986 to about 3% in 1991. 
The reopening of the local oil refinery, once a major source of employment and 
foreign exchange earnings, promises to give the economy an additional boost.
