ECONOMY 
Overview: Subsistence agriculture provides the main livelihood for 80-90% 
of the population, but accounts for less than 15% of GDP. Oil production is 
imported. For the long run, Angola has the advantage of rich natural resources 
in addition to oil, notably gold, diamonds, and arable land. To realize its 
economic potential Angola not only must secure domestic peace but also must 
reform government policies that have led to distortions and imbalances 
throughout the economy.
