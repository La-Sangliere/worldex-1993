ECONOMY 
Overview: The economy depends primarily on petroleum, phosphates, tourism, 
and exports of light manufactures. Following two years of drought-induced 
unemployment has eroded popular support for the government, however, and 
forced Tunis to slow the pace of economic reform. Nonetheless, the government 
appears committed to implementing its IMF-supported structural adjustment 
program and to servicing its foreign debt.
