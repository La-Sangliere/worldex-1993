ECONOMY 
Overview: The country has few national resources. Commercially viable 
phosphate deposits were exhausted at the time of independence in 1979. Copra 
and fish now represent the bulk of production and exports. The economy has 
fluctuated widely in recent years. Real GDP declined about 8% in 1987, as the 
fish catch fell sharply to only one-fourth the level of 1986 and copra 
increase in copra production and a good fish catch. Following the strong surge 
in output in 1988, GNP increased 1% in both 1989 and 1990.
