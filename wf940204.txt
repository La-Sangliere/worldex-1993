ECONOMY 
Overview: In this small, open, tropical island economy, the tourist 
industry employs about 30% of the labor force and provides more than 70% of 
hard currency earnings. In recent years the government has encouraged foreign 
investment in order to upgrade hotels and other services. At the same time, 
the government has moved to reduce the high dependence on tourism by promoting 
the development of farming, fishing, and small-scale manufacturing.
